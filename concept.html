Concept

The Giuntru project unfolds through cycles: inner states, shifting forms, and the quiet mechanics of change. Each release is a chapter, but also a snapshot, a way to hold an experience without explaining it too much. The sound moves between intimacy and space, atmosphere and structure, always seeking clarity: not perfection, but presence.
What matters here is not genre. It is direction.

Origin

It began with a relationship unfolding across many years. Watching someone grow, disappear, return, and grow again changes the way you understand time. There was laughter, trust, small rituals, and the quiet sense of being understood without effort.

At some point, I felt the need to leave something behind, not a dedication in plain words, but a trace. Something that could stand on its own, even after the moment that shaped it had passed.
Silent Waves was born from that impulse.

The Becoming Cycle

The first four albums trace an inner passage, a sequence of states rather than themes.
From protection to desire. From desire to transformation. From transformation to stillness.
Not a narrative arc, but a shift in density: the sound of existence learning how to stay, move, dissolve, and simply be.

1 Silent Waves

Silent Waves is the starting point: a gentle emergence. It carries quiet affection, protection, and the first light of something that chooses to remain. Not by force, but by resonance. Here, the project begins to breathe.

2 Lucid Dream

Lucid Dream moves into closeness: body, desire, and the strange softness of being awake inside a dream. Intimacy becomes a space rather than a performance, where emotions and sensations can be trusted.

3 Pulse Becoming

Pulse Becoming is movement and transformation. Identity loosens, forms blur, and what once felt stable becomes fluid. Becoming is not a goal, but a process unfolding.

4 Just Being

Just Being is release. Less narrative, less grip, less need to prove. It rests in presence and silence, in the clarity that appears when the moment is no longer pushed forward.

Five

Then the axis changes. What was inward now turns outward.
It is no longer about holding a state, but entering a relation. Not defined by structure, but by interaction. Not controlled, but felt. Each track exists as a different condition: a moment of contact, tension, grounding, expansion, and dissolution. The sound is allowed to shift, to break coherence, because coherence is no longer the goal. What matters is what happens between.

AI in the Process

AI is part of the production, but not the source of meaning. In its fully generative form, AI can replace authorship, with its strengths and its limits. Here it is used differently: as an executive force that helps develop and perform an idea. Concept, direction, lyrics, melodic composition, and stylistic framework remain human. The aesthetic boundaries are deliberately defined: vocal clarity, controlled harmonic density, minimal noise, and restrained texture form the core of the sonic language.
AI accelerates iteration, expands possibilities, and supports technical execution, like working with a band of highly versatile professionals. The authorship stays in the decisions.

Human Direction

  • Concept and artistic vision
  • Lyrics and narrative structure
  • Melodic themes and harmonic progressions
  • Structural composition
  • Stylistic framework and aesthetic constraints
  • Post-processing and vocal refinement
  • Noise reduction and clarity control
  • Final selection and coherence control

AI Contribution

  • Arrangement and production layering
  • Rapid iteration and variation
  • Sound design assistance
  • Technical execution support

Future Works

This page describes a living structure, not a closed catalogue. More cycles will come: albums, short collections, and experiments that may move across styles while keeping the same underlying intent, translating experience into sound with as little noise as possible.
